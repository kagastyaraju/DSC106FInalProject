Standing Up: How Your Body Keeps You Steady

Ever felt dizzy after standing up too fast? Let’s dive into the hidden dance of your heart, lungs, and brain that keeps you upright—most of the time.

Introduction

Your body is a symphony of systems—your heart pumps blood, your lungs breathe air, and your brain keeps everything in check, all working seamlessly together. But when you stand up suddenly, gravity pulls blood downward, challenging this harmony and risking a dizzy spell. Through this interactive journey, explore real data to see how your body fights back, adjusting in seconds to keep you steady.
